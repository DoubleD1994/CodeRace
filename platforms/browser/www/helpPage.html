How To Play

CodeRace is a location-based game for teams. To play, you go to locations shown on the map, solve the clues and claim the locations by entering the right code. The team with the most location markers at the end are the winners.

Key

The main game interface is provided by Google Maps including the standard controls:

  • - Zoom In
  • - Zoom Out

The other controls on the CodeRace main screen are:


  • - Player Location

  • - Same Team Players

  • - Other Team Players

  • - Easy Claimable Location, worth 1 point

  • - Medium Claimable Location, worth 2 points

  • - Hard Claimable Location, worth 3 points

  • - Point Swing Claimable Location, worth 3 points, other teams lose 2 points

  • - Hide Maps Claimable Location, worth 1 point, other teams map not visible for 60 seconds

  • - Claimed Location

  • - Get Help

  • - Check Score

  • - Enter Chat

  • - Show Other Players

  • - Hide Other Players

  • - Log Out
